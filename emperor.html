How the Emperor Share System Works
1. Your Main Code
Your permanent login. Format: name-xxxxx. Keep it safe.
2. Emperor Code
The code issued when you earned your 49th medal. This logs you into this portal only. Never share it.
3. Tier Codes
Each tier (STR, BRZ, SLV, GLD, ELT) has its own share code. Give that code to friends to confirm. When they do, your tier count rises.
4. Rewards Auto-Unlock
When your tier fills up, Puzzlr detects it and unlocks your reward automatically next time you go online in the game.
5. Emperor Status
As Emperor, the public share site redirects to your portal. Your identity is protected — the public sees only the gate.
